About Me

Outside of class I am involved in many organizations.

  • Every fall that I have been at Michigan I perform as a member of the trumpet section with the Michigan Marching Band at all home football games!
  • Every winter that I have been at Michigan I perform at the Men’s and Women’s Basketball games as a member of the Band and travel with the teams to various post-season tournaments!
  • Every spring for the past three years I have coached the JV Softball Team at Pioneer High School where I played for the previous four seasons!
  • I stay involved at the Synagogue where I grew up by teaching at the Religious School. Last year I taught the 8th Grade and this year I teach 6th Grade!
